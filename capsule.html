about device

AIREGO is the project that will help to spread information, about air pollution on our planet, also it will help our population to take action to solve the problems in their regions.

AIREGO basically is a tool that will be placed in parks, its close to touchscreen ad will provide lots of options. First of all, it will change the color based on the air condition around it, so it always shows if there is any problem. Secondly, it has an option not only to give out information but also people can buy and plant trees, bring and test air from any place, even from their home.
